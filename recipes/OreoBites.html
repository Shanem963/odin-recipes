Oreo Bites

This is a recipe on how to make Oreo Bites.

Ingredients

  • 30g protein powder, chocolate, vanilla or unflavoured
  • 50g cream cheese
  • 3 oreo biscuits
  • 20g 70% dark chocolate

Cooking instructions

  1. Place the protein powder, cream cheese, oreo biscuits and 3 tbsp water into a food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Roll the mixture into 12 equal-sized balls and leave on a plate in the fridge to firm up.
  3. Melt the dark chocolate then drizzle over the bites. Place back in the fridge for 20 minutes or until set.
