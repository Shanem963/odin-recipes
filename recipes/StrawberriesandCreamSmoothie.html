Strawberries and Cream Smoothie

This is a recipe on how to make Strawberries and Cream Smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 100g yoghurt, such as greek, natural, soya
  • 80g strawberries
  • 50ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 30g protein powder, vanilla, strawberry or unflavoured
  • 30g almond butter
  • 20g strawberry jam
  • 1 handful of ice

Cooking instructions

  1. Combine everything in a liquidiser and blend until smooth.
